POINT-A POINT-B



POINT-A POINT-B is an interactive installation that opens space for meaningful intercommunication between human beings and non-human beings. This ultimately is meant to take away labels between different forms of existence on earth and allow for relationships of energy– releasing us from the definitive nature of man-made language and instead encouraging universal language as well as the acknowledgement and appreciation of other beings: their complexity, cognizance and actuality. The alligator clips and nails can be placed into the soil of any plant, and depending on the plant’s needs, conditions and subsequent space of existence, the circuit will play a different sound and have a different phrase displayed. The conditions can change if the plant is watered, left in the sun, or even if someone talks near the plant– all resulting in the sounds and display changing. Ultimately, POINT-A POINT-B is meant to comment on the simplicity of straightforward communication – extending it to humans and all living beings in this universe alike.

Exhibited in the Ecogenic Research Collective’s 'First Instar' NYC 2022








Created with P5js, Raspberry PI, Adafruit Clue, electronic components, plaster and plants - 2022
